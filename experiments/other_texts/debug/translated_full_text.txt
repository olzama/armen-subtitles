To analyze. Why is this episode special and how does what we call art work within it? For our first experience of this kind, we chose one of the most eerie yet interesting scenes from Fyodor Dostoevsky's novel "Demons". This episode is from almost the very end of the book, from its final part. In the scene, a certain Verkhovensky comes at night to the house of a certain Kirillov and sees a chicken cooked with rice on the window. And although there is a loaded gun lying right next to the chicken, it is the chicken, not the gun, that takes on the role of Chekhov's gun, which is about to go off. However, to understand what this shot is and whom it is aimed at, one must first figure out who these two are and how they ended up together at night in a room in the annex.
