Language quality requirements for Galician:
- Write native-level Galician as a fluent L1 speaker would.
- Use clitic pronouns correctly according to native Galician usage (placement, form, and combinations).
- Avoid castellanismos: do not import Spanish vocabulary, morphology, or constructions when a genuine Galician equivalent exists.
- Do not invent words that merely look Galician but that are not actually used in the language.
